About Me


This web site is about me, Selwyn C. Williams Jr. It is an informational page about who I am. I would like to give some background information about me and about some of the things that I enjoy.


I was born in St. Louis, MO, in 1981, but I have spent my life living in Illinois. I grew up as the oldest of four children to Annette and Selwyn Williams Sr. We grew up in East St. Louis, which is where my parents reside today.


The name "Selwyn" means friend of the manor, or loosely translated to mean, good friend. My grandmother got the name from a woman who was pregnant at the same time that my grandmother was. This woman was going to name her child, Selwyn, if she had a boy. The woman had a girl, but my grandmother told her that since she was having a son that she would use the name. They remain in contact for years after this.


I have been currently married for 8 years to a lovely lady named Erin. We have one child who is named Selwyn III, also known as "3-peat"
